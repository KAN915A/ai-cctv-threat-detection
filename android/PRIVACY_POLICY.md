# Privacy Policy — AI CCTV

_Last updated: 11 July 2026_

AI CCTV ("the app") is a viewer for a self-hosted AI camera-detection server
that you, the user, run and control. This policy explains what the app does
and does not do with your data.

## Data the app collects

**None is sent to us or any third party.** The developer operates no servers
and receives no data from the app.

All app configuration (your server address, camera source, and preferences)
is stored locally on your device using Android's private app storage.

## Data the app accesses

- **Your detection server**, at the address you enter in Settings. The app
  requests live video frames, threat status, and alert history from that
  server over your local network. That server is operated by you, not by us.
- **Camera video** is processed entirely by your own server. Video is never
  routed through, stored by, or accessible to the developer.

## Permissions

- **Internet / Network state** — to reach the detection server you configure.
- **Notifications** — to alert you when a HIGH or CRITICAL threat is detected.
- **Foreground service** — optional background monitoring that keeps checking
  your server for threats while the app is closed. You can turn this off in
  Settings; it is off by default.

## Data sharing

The app shares no personal data, video, or usage data with any third party.
There is no analytics SDK, no advertising, and no tracking.

## Data retention and deletion

The app stores only your settings, locally. Uninstalling the app removes
them. Alert history and snapshots live on your own detection server; manage
or delete them there.

## Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no personal data from
anyone.
